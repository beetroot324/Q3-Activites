Moon Knight the Series:


Moon Knight

The story of Moon Knight centers on Steven Grant, a mild-mannered gift shop employee who suffers from blackouts and recollections of a previous life. After learning he has dissociative identity disorder, Steven and mercenary Marc Spector learn they share a body.





Konshu

The Egyptian moon and sky deity Khonshu. He was exiled from the Ennead Council, but not before he had used his Avatars, the Moon Knights, to exact revenge on those who had injured innocent people. He bestows Moon Knight, Marc Spector, and Steven Grant with their abilities.






Scarlet Scarab

Scarlet Scarab, also known as Layla El-Faouly, makes her MCU debut in the Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice film, Moon Knight. She is the love of Moon Knight's life and an ally in his struggle against Arthur Harrow and Ammit. Layla is amiable, kind, and soft-spoken. She gains superpowers in the form of an Egyptian goddess when she prays to Taweret.
