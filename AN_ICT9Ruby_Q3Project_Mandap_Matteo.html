Fire Safety Quiz

Directions: This is a quiz to see if you are prepared if a fire breaks out in your house. If all of ur answers are YES then you are prepared and safe. If more than 3 answers is NO then you have to follow what the question is asking for.

1. Does Your home have more than 1 ways of exiting the premises?




2. Do you know the Emergency hotlines that are near your area?



3. Do you have a fully working fire extinguisher?



4. Does your home have many easy ways of accessing water?



5. Do you live with anyone who have disablitites? If yes, will you be able to assist them in case of a emergency?



6. If a fire breaks out near your house, will u be able to assist those who are in danger?



7. Do you have smoke detectors installed in your home?



8. Are you a light sleeper? If you are not, please be prepared for anything happening while your sleeping



9. Will you be able to ask for help from residences near you?



10. Are you physically and mentally fit for the things that might happen if a fire breaks out in your home?




Do you have any suggestions or though about our quiz? If you do please feel free to write it down!
