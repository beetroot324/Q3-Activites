My Top 3 Movies OAT

Star Wars Episode III - (Revenge of the Sith)
Director - George Lucas
Date Released - May 19, 2005 		Synopsis -
The Clone Wars have been going on for three years now. Chancellor Palpatine is saved by Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi and Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker from the droid armies' leader, General Grievous, but Grievous manages to get away. Chancellor Palpatine, with whom Anakin has established a bond, has come under suspicion from the Jedi Council. Anakin is asked to eavesdrop on the chancellor and is so resentful of the Jedi Council that he turns to the Dark Side.
Barcelona: A Love Untold
Director - Olivia Lamasan
Date Released - September 14, 2016		 Synopsis -
While attending university in Spain, a young architecture student dealing with grief encounters a fellow expat trying to leave her past behind. She has encountered a young man on her journey away from her troubled past who will end up being an important support system for both her and her loved ones when she returns.
The Wolf of Wall Street
Director - Martin Scorsese
Date Released - January 15, 2014		 Synopsis -
This is a fictionalized account of the real-life events that led Jordan Belfort from affluence and luxury as a stock trader to his downfall at the hands of crime, corruption, and the federal government.
